Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

In short

  • Shooal is a personal dive-logging journal. You sign in, log the marine life you spot, and appear on a leaderboard.
  • We collect your email (or Apple sign-in), your username, the content you create, a few optional onboarding answers, and anonymous usage analytics.
  • We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all its data at any time from inside the app.
  • You can turn usage analytics off in Settings → Privacy.

01Who we are

This app and service ("Shooal", "we", "us") is operated by Shooal, based in Nijmegen, The Netherlands. We are the data controller responsible for your personal data under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

For any privacy question or request, contact us at jiddewiegertjes@gmail.com.

02What we collect

We only collect what the app needs to work and to improve.

DataWhat it is
AccountYour email address (for a one-time sign-in code) or your Apple sign-in identifier if you use Sign in with Apple.
ProfileThe username you choose, shown on the leaderboard.
Your contentThe species you log, your favourites/wishlist, your planned trips ("journeys"), and any species requests you submit.
Onboarding surveyOptional answers to how you heard about Shooal, your age range, and your gender. You may skip all of these.
Usage analyticsAnonymous, aggregated product events (e.g. "logged a species", "opened the leaderboard") and basic device/app information, used to understand how Shooal is used and to improve it.

We do not collect your precise location, your photos, your contacts, or any advertising identifier (IDFA). We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.

03How we use your data & why

  • To run the service — sign you in, save your collection, sync it across your devices, and show the leaderboard. Legal basis: performance of our agreement with you.
  • To send your sign-in code — a transactional email containing your one-time login code. Legal basis: performance of our agreement with you.
  • To improve Shooal — understand which features are used and where people get stuck, using analytics. Legal basis: your consent (you can opt out at any time) / legitimate interest in improving the product.
  • To understand our audience — the optional survey answers help us learn who uses Shooal. Legal basis: your consent (these questions are optional).

05Where your data is stored

We aim to keep personal data within the European Union where possible. Your account and content are stored in our database hosted in the European Union (Ireland), and our analytics provider (PostHog) is hosted in the European Union. Some providers — such as our email-delivery service and Sign in with Apple — may process limited data outside the EU; where that happens, it is protected by appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

06How long we keep it

We keep your account and content for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, your account and all associated data (profile, sightings, favourites, journeys, species requests, and survey answers) are permanently removed from our database. Analytics events are retained in aggregate and are not used to re-identify you after deletion.

07Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. Specifically with Shooal:

  • Delete everything — open Settings → Delete account in the app to permanently erase your account and all its data.
  • Turn off analytics — toggle Share usage analytics off in Settings → Privacy.
  • Access or correct your data — email us at jiddewiegertjes@gmail.com.
  • Withdraw consent — where we rely on consent (analytics, survey answers), you can withdraw it at any time without affecting earlier processing.

08Children

Shooal is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

09Security

Access to your data is protected by authentication and database-level security rules, so each account can only read and write its own data. No method of transmission or storage is ever completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

10Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we'll update the "last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

11Contact & complaints

Questions or requests? Email jiddewiegertjes@gmail.com.

If you are in the EU and believe we have not handled your data properly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In the Netherlands this is the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens.
